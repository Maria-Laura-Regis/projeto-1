What is Software?

The software is responsible for making the machine understand and execute user commands. It is classified as the logical and immaterial part, whose function is to provide instructions to the hardware. Hardware is the physical part of the device.


Web Softwares

There are software hosted on online servers and that can be accessed through internet browsers, such as Microsoft Edge, Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome, among others. They usually have similar features to other programs, however, do not need to download and require internet connection to be used. They are called Web Softwares.


The Job of a Web Developer

The Web Developer perform the technical and visual development of web pages and maintenance of websites, defining languages, databases, storage and updating of information in order to meet the volume of Internet users and its correct operation.
